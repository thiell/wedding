BOD

The closest airport to Arcachon and Biscarrosse, the towns on Arcachon Bay where the wedding events will take place.

  • You can fly to BOD from Paris and other cities in Europe.
  • Car rental: There are many car rental agencies at the airport.
  • Public transit: A tram connects BOD to downtown Bordeaux and Bordeaux Saint-Jean train station. From there you can take a local train to Arcachon.
  • Other: Uber and taxis serve the airport, or you can book a car in advance. The MonTransport website compares rates and lists local taxi apps that offer advance scheduling.
  • BOD is about an hour's drive from Arcachon, 1.25 hours from Biscarrosse, and 30 minutes from Bordeaux Old Town (without traffic).

Paris Charles-de-Gaulle Airport
CDG

  • High-speed TGV trains connect CDG with Bordeaux Saint-Jean train station. It takes 2-3 hours, depending on the number of stops.
  • Train tickets can be booked on the SNCF website. They often add more train options closer to the date of travel.
  • There are many airport hotels if you need to stay a night at CDG. We stayed at ibis Styles Paris CDG Airport and found it perfectly fine for one night and very convenient.

Bordeaux Saint-Jean Station
Gare SNCF Bordeaux Saint-Jean

High speed trains connect Bordeaux with Paris and take 2-3 hours depending on the number of stops. Paris departure stations are Paris-Montparnasse in central Paris or Charles de Gaulle Airport. All end at Bordeaux Saint-Jean station.

Arcachon Station
Gare SNCF d'Arcachon

Local trains take about an hour to go between Bordeaux Saint-Jean and Arcachon train station. The Arcachon train station is in easy walking distance to Arcachon Town Hall (our civil ceremony location), the departure for the Arcachon Bay boat tour, and many hotels, restaurants, and shops.

üöó Rental car

In Arcachon and around, a rental car is suggested. You can rent in Bordeaux or at the Arcachon train station.

In Bordeaux, it is not recommended to rent a car unless you are traveling outside the city. Central Bordeaux is very walkable, and the local tram system provides good coverage for longer distances.
